Privacy Policy

This policy explains how LocalApex ("we", "us", "our"), a product of Maveric Infotech, collects, uses, and protects your information — including data accessed via the Google Business Profile APIs.

🔵

Google API Services User Data Policy

LocalApex's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Google Business Profile data is used only to provide the features described in this document and on our product page.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you register for LocalApex, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Business name and type
  • Password (stored as a bcrypt hash — never in plain text)

1.2 Google Business Profile Data (via Google API)

When you connect your Google Business Profile, we access the following data through the business.manage OAuth 2.0 scope:

  • Performance metrics: search impressions, website clicks, direction requests, phone calls (read-only)
  • Reviews: review text, star ratings, reviewer names (read), and owner replies (write, user-triggered)
  • Business information: name, address, phone number, website URL, business hours, categories, description, photos (read + write, user-triggered)
  • Account and location data: GBP account IDs and linked location names (read-only, for account selection)
  • Notification settings: alert email addresses for review and Q&A notifications (read + write, user-triggered)
  • Place Action links: booking and reservation URLs on the listing (read + write, user-triggered)
  • Verification status: whether the location is verified on Google (read-only)
  • Lodging attributes: amenities, check-in times, room features for hospitality businesses (read + write, user-triggered)

We access this data only when you, the authenticated account owner, trigger a specific action within LocalApex. No background polling or speculative data collection occurs.

1.3 Usage Data

We collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps) for security monitoring and service improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the LocalApex dashboard and all its features
  • To display your Google Business Profile analytics, reviews, and listing information
  • To publish review replies and profile updates to Google on your behalf when you click "Send" or "Save"
  • To generate your 13-point SEO audit score and improvement recommendations
  • To send you product notifications and account-related emails
  • To improve our services (using aggregated, anonymised data only)

We do not use your Google Business Profile data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose not listed above.

3. Limited Use — Google API Data

In compliance with Google's Limited Use requirements, LocalApex's use of data obtained from Google APIs is limited to:

  • Providing and improving features explicitly described on localapex.mavericinfotech.in
  • Displaying information to the authenticated user who granted access
  • Actions explicitly initiated by the authenticated user

LocalApex does NOT:

  • Sell Google API data to any third party
  • Use Google API data for serving advertisements
  • Allow humans to read user data except where necessary to provide the service, to comply with law, or with explicit user consent
  • Transfer Google API data to third parties except as necessary to provide the service

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers hosted in India (Hostinger infrastructure). We implement the following security measures:

  • Passwords stored using bcrypt hashing (never in plain text)
  • Google credentials are never stored — only OAuth 2.0 refresh tokens
  • All API communications over HTTPS/TLS
  • JWT-based session tokens with expiry
  • Database access restricted to application servers only

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or Google Business Profile data with third parties, except:

  • Service providers: Infrastructure (Hostinger), email delivery — bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or valid legal process
  • Business transfer: If Maveric Infotech is acquired or merged, under equivalent privacy protections

6. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update your account information at any time in Settings
  • Deletion: Disconnect your Google account and request full data deletion via Settings → Connected Accounts → Disconnect, or by emailing us. We will delete all your data within 30 days.
  • Revoke Google access: You can revoke LocalApex's access to your Google account at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions

7. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained for the duration of your subscription and for 30 days after deletion request
  • Google API data cached for performance is retained for a maximum of 24 hours
  • Server logs are retained for 90 days

8. Cookies

LocalApex uses only essential cookies (session management, CSRF protection). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

LocalApex is intended for business use by adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or an in-app banner. Continued use of LocalApex after changes constitutes acceptance.
